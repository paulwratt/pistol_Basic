* Guild details
- Rangers - discount on Tools and Archery items
- Rangers - Law: improve Nature Knowledge
- Rangers - Fletchers: produce Bows and Arrows
- Rangers + Masons = WatchTower: improves Hunting, adds to Defences 
- Rangers + Priests = Beekeepers: produces Honey
- Rangers + Priests + Farmers = Vinyard: produce Grapes
- Rangers + Miners = Survey: allows detailed and better investigation
- Rangers + Miners + Masons = Waterpump: produce water without a tile
- Rangers + Mages = Naturalists: produce Potion ingredience, improves Law
